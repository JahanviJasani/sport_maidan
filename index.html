Schoolexcursions

Sports help in one’s learning life skills and physical development, however, Due to lack of interest, interesting sport options, or lack of knowledge, students are not willingly participating in sports today.

To address this, Sports Maidan has created a sports awareness cum interactive day tour program where children of ages 8+, get introduced to 20+ covered in ‘khelo India’ and summer Olympics.

SPORTS MAIDAN is a 15acre sports hub at Gurugram. Our programs are experiential where children interact with sports trainers, professional players, and experience the sport and get first-hand experience and knowledge about different games and their prospects. All the activities are provided under professional supervision.
